About Me

Libo Huang is an assistant researcher at the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) in China. Before that, he worked as a postdoctoral researcher (2021.10 - 2024.8) and intern researcher (2021.7-2021.10) at CAS, collaborated with Prof. Yongjun Xu and Prof. Zhulin An, working on deep learning.

He got his Ph.D. degree (2018-2021) in machine learning under the supervision of Prof. Wing-Kuen Ling at Guangdong University of Technology (GDUT), and Prof. Lu Gan at Brunel University London (2019-2020).

Before that, he pursued his M.Eng. degree (2016-2018) in Computer Scicence from GDUT, supervised by Prof. Zhifeng Hao, Prof. Ruichu Cai, and Prof. Wen Wen. He received a B.S. degree (2012-2016) in Mathematics from Jiangxi Normal Unversity (JXNU).

Research Interests

My research interests include machine learning theories and deep learning applications. I have done some work on the following topics:
  • Machine learning, especially unsupervised clustering, feature extracture, and optimization.
  • Deep learning, especially the deep models' memorizing, etc.

Recruiting: I have several 1-2 openings for visiting (bachelor, master, or Ph.D.) students on machine learning, deep learning, and optimization topics. Both remote and onsite visitings are welcome. Drop me an email if you are interested!
